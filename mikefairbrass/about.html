About

After over twenty years as Head of Modelmaking and Photography with Richard Rogers, Mike is uniquely placed to offer a range of creative consultancy and teaching across design, including bespoke In-house model making workshops, model making recruitment and studio photography.

He promotes making through teaching in architectural practices and at BA hons degree and MA levels. He has devised and hosted educational workshops at the Victoria and Albert Museum, Design Museum, the Royal Academy and for the Sorrell Foundation Masterclass series.

He has exhibition design and Installation experience in London, Europe and the Far East and has written for the Architects Journal. He continues to write columns and books. He loves creative ideas and projects and is always keen to broaden his experience into new areas.
