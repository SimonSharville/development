If You Could Collaborate is the fourth annual If You Could exhibition. Organised by It’s Nice That.

Aiming to provide a platform for the finest creatives from all over the world to question their conventional working methods and outcomes. The contributors have been challenged to produce something a little unexpected, by working with a partner of their choosing from any discipline, profession or background. There is no brief to answer, or format to honour – the only limit being the enterprise and imagination of the artists involved.

AAvec is a font inspired by building floor plates, which we called Floorplay. These elements were installed together as an exhibition: The physical scale model, an interpretation of some letters of this font, spelling the word AVEC (french for WITH, opposite of SANS - Without). A neon sign. Photographs of the models, the different perspectives bringing out the collaborative aspect of the project. 26 laser cut letters. The font was used for all the text related to the project.
