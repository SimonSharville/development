The Little Book of Scale

If the Sun is the size of a Grapefruit, the Earth is a grain of sand, and the distance between the two is a London bus.

Scale is intriguing. Scale is everywhere. Scale is our experience of the world. The human scale is the 1:1, the point of reference. Everything is designed around it. Wealth is an example of scale, so is a sculpture, a building, a planet or a molecule.

Scale is a universal and timeless subject. It's a BIG subject. We aim to celebrate it and distil facts to fit our little book without losing their strength.
